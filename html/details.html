The plan

  • Where is it?

    We'll be at the Ridgewood Event Center in Hesperus, about a half hour's drive from Durango.

  • How do I get there?

    We plan on running a bus there and back from downtown Durango — please fill out our survey to let us know whether or not you're planning on riding the bus! It will pick up and drop off at the Durango Transit Center, and there's ample (free) parking there as well.

  • What time is it?

    We'll start at 4 o'clock, but you should plan on arriving a little early to take a stroll around the venue and take in the incredible views of the La Platas. Dinner will be around 6, and we'll wrap up by 10.

  • Dress code?

    Wear your get up to get down. In other words, let's dance! We'll be outside on soft ground, so wear comfortable shoes.

  • Can my kids come?

    We will give them a balloon and unlimited Shirley Temples (seriously).

  • Where should we stay?

    We're working on putting together a block of rooms in a few different hotels around the area. There's also plenty of Airbnb/VRBO options, as well as campgrounds for the adventurous and cheap.
